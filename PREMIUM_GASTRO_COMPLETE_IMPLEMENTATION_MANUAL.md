## 2. PSYCHOLOGICAL FOUNDATION

### **CORE PSYCHOLOGICAL PRINCIPLES**

#### **2.1 Fear + Reward Psychology**
- **Amygdala Response**: 3x stronger reaction to negative stimuli (fear) than positive
- **Loss Aversion**: People fight harder to avoid losing status than to gain money
- **NASRAT Tier**: Hidden punishment system creates fear-based compliance
- **Status Rewards**: Recognition-based benefits trigger dopamine without costing money

#### **2.2 The 16 Motivator System (Reiss Profile)**
Based on Professor Steven Reiss's research with 6,000+ subjects across four continents:

1. **POWER** - Want to control, lead, dominate
2. **STATUS** - Want recognition, prestige, social standing  
3. **INDEPENDENCE** - Want autonomy, self-reliance
4. **CURIOSITY** - Want to learn, discover, explore
5. **ACCEPTANCE** - Want to belong, be liked, fit in
6. **ORDER** - Want structure, organization, predictability
7. **SAVING** - Want to collect, preserve resources
8. **HONOR** - Want to be moral, ethical, do right thing
9. **IDEALISM** - Want to improve society, make world better
10. **SOCIAL CONTACT** - Want interaction, relationships, networking
11. **FAMILY** - Want to protect and nurture family/team
12. **VENGEANCE** - Want to compete, win, defeat others
13. **ROMANCE/BEAUTY** - Want aesthetic experience, beauty
14. **EATING** - Want culinary excellence, taste experiences
15. **PHYSICAL ACTIVITY** - Want movement, energy, dynamic experiences
16. **TRANQUILITY** - Want peace, calm, stress reduction

#### **2.3 Choice Psychology**
- **Paradox of Choice**: 2-3 options optimal (too many overwhelm, too few feel forced)
- **Illusion of Control**: When customers "choose," they feel ownership of decision
- **Effort Justification**: Customers value rewards they work for more highly

#### **2.4 Social Media Psychology**
- **Unfiltered Profile**: Social media reveals true psychological state without business filters
- **Status Signaling**: Posts reveal what they want others to think about them
- **Competitive Behavior**: Shows their rivals, insecurities, aspirations
- **Real-time Emotional State**: Current posts indicate optimal approach timing
